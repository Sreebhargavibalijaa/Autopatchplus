Frequently Asked Questions

How does Autopatch+ detect hallucinations?

+

We use a multi-model verification system that cross-references claims against trusted knowledge sources, analyzes semantic consistency, and evaluates statement confidence levels. Our proprietary algorithms can detect both factual inaccuracies and logical inconsistencies with 99.7% accuracy.

What LLMs do you support?

+

Autopatch+ works with all major LLMs including GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, LLaMA, Mistral, and others. Our system is model-agnostic and can be integrated with any text-generating AI system through our API or SDK.

How much latency does Autopatch+ add?

+

Our optimized verification pipeline adds an average of just 150ms to response times. For applications where speed is critical, we offer a streaming mode that begins verification while the LLM is still generating content.

Can I use my own knowledge base?

+

Yes! Autopatch+ allows you to integrate your proprietary knowledge bases, documentation, and approved content sources. Our system will prioritize your internal knowledge when verifying and patching content.
